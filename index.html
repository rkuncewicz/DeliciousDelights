    Welcome to Delicious Delights

    You seem hungry, here let me help. This is an application that allows you, the user, to search for a recipe that is desirable. Organized nicely by showing the pictures of the delicious meals, you can choose what tickles your fancy. It also works with ingredients, so if that squash you bought a few weeks ago isn't looking too great, you may want to type it in and find something to make with it.
